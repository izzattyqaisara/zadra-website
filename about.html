About Zadra Ingénieurs Sdn Bhd

Zadra Ingénieurs Sdn Bhd (formerly Zadra Consultant) is a Kuching-based Civil & Structural Engineering consultancy, established on 12 December 2006. We partner closely with clients as part of the design team, balancing functionality, economy, and aesthetics to deliver robust, cost-effective solutions.

  • Location: Kuching, Sarawak
  • Focus: Civil & Structural engineering consultancy
  • Approach: Team collaboration, BIM-enabled workflows, value engineering

Core Expertise

  • Project master planning & feasibility
  • Detailed design (civil, structural & infrastructure)
  • Contract administration & tender support
  • Construction supervision & quality control

Sectors & Specialisation

  • Airports: runways, taxiways, aprons; hangars & general aviation
  • Roads, highways, bridges & drainage/irrigation
  • Sewerage & public infrastructure
  • Educational & institutional buildings
  • Industrial & land development, sand filling works
  • Jetties & wharfs

Design Services

  • Structural analysis & design (reinforced concrete & steel)
  • Hydraulic/drainage design & flood management
  • Sewerage network & treatment interfacing
  • Airside/landside airport infrastructure planning
  • Road geometry, pavement & earthworks

How We Deliver

  • BIM-enabled coordination & clash resolution
  • Modern tools, codes & best practices
  • Cost, programme & risk-conscious solutions
  • Clear reporting & stakeholder communication
