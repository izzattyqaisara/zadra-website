Company Brief

Zadra Ingénieurs Sdn. Bhd. (previously Zadra Consultant) was established in Kuching, Sarawak on 12 December 2006 to provide consultancy services in Civil & Structural Engineering.

Our team brings broad experience across public infrastructure and building projects, with a reputation for integrity and quality of work.

Track record includes master planning, contract administration and supervision for airports, hangars, general aviation, infrastructure, sand filling works, schools, institutional buildings, runways, taxiways & parking aprons.

We work collaboratively with clients as part of the design team—aiming for the optimum balance of functionality, economy and aesthetics using modern materials, sound engineering practice and up-to-date tools.

What we do

  • Civil & Structural design from feasibility to detailed design
  • Peer review / independent check and QA/QC gateways
  • Contract administration & site support
  • BIM coordination for clash detection and model-based quantities
